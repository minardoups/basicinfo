PCLC 1. Confirm receipt of hardware

- TSG to acknowledge receipt of all PCLC PC hardware and peripherals.

- Keep accurate QTY received notes in task until 100%

- DPC must be provided with accurate HW counts.

- Review with supervisor before closing task.

- Do not close task until ALL equipment has been received.

PCLC 3. Decommission

- Handover of new device to the user and removal of old PC.

- Disk Wipe PC prior to E- recycling.

- E-Recycle (SOCS) PC.

- Update cost code information in EARS (REQUIRED - DO NOT CLOSE TASK UNTIL COST CODE VERIFIED AND ENTERED INTO EARS!)

- Decommission PCLC target from Active Directory.

PCLC 2. Provisioning / Migration

- Build device based on target information.

Use EARS to identify workstation, APP ID, location, and user/owner of target on Box/Label.

- Contact user, verify any additional equipment needs, and schedule migration.

- Migrate user data. Ensure disk encryption is 100% before handing over to user.

- Once Task 2 is closed complete, tech to complete Cost Code Questionnaire.

PCLC 4. PCLC Installation Photos

- TSG to take photos of final Desktop PC placement

- Photo detail to contain Straight on, Above, & Bottom (Below desk) views.

- Attach photos (Paper clip) within SMC WOT.

- Photos of laptops / remote desktop setups not required (update note & close task).
